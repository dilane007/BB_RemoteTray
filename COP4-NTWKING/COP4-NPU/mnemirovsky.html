Experiences & Achievements

Major contributions 

·      Lead the way on Massively Multithreading architectures (MMT)
Word developed at FlowStorm in 2001 and first product in 2005 at ConSentry Networks Inc.

·       First to introduce SMT for packet processing
The CNP810SP at Clearwater Networks

·       Pioneer of Simultaneous Multithreading (SMT)
(originally published in Micro-24 in 1991 under the term “dynamic multi-streaming”), subsequent results in HICSS (1993 and 1994), and in PACT (1995)

·       Pioneer of real-time multithreading microprocessor
on the TIO engine controller of General Motors-Delphi, released 1991, over 50,000,000 units sold

·        Founder/CTO of 3 USA companies 
ConSentry Networks, Flowstorm and XStream Logic

·        Holder of 60 US patents
